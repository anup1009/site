Those hard fought moments in my life brought me a strong desire to suceed in my life and i am on the way to achieve it.Although being in a middle class family is always tough i didnt face too much problems but was a victim of different racisms and discrimination. I had completed my School leaving certificate from Gandaki Higher Secondary Boarding School (GHSBS) which is a very famous school in my country and completed my +2 education from Motherland Secondary School. Now i am currently involved in High school education at Paschimanchal Campus of Tribhuvan University loated in my own city of Pokhara.
